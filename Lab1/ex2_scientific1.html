1. Challenge:¶

Random display $N=50$ circles:

  • Center $c_i = (x_i, y_i)$ random with $x_i, y_i \in \left [0 , 1 \right]$
  • Color $color_i$ random with $color_i \in \left[0 , 1\right]$
  • Area $area_i = \pi * r_i^2$ with random generation $r_i \in \left[0, 1\right]$

%time
# Generate randomly the center (x, y) of the circle, color, area
N = 50
# rand(N): generate N numbers from [0; 1)
x = np.random.rand(N)
y = np.random.rand(N)
colors = np.random.rand(N)

# Radius of circle - [0; 15)
pixel_point = 15
area = np.pi * (pixel_point * np.random.rand(N)) ** 2
